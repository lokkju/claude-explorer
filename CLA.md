When you open your first pull request against the project, [CLA Assistant](https://cla-assistant.io) will comment on the PR with a one-click signing link. The signing flow uses your GitHub identity — no separate account or email exchange required. Your signature is recorded once and applies to all future contributions to this project.

If you're contributing on behalf of a corporate entity that requires its own CCLA (Corporate Contributor License Agreement) signed before any of its employees can contribute, please email the maintainer to arrange the paperwork; the ICLA above is for individual contributors only.
